UX Designer Roadmap

Your guide to becoming a successful UX designer

1. Introduction to UX Design

Start by understanding the fundamental concepts of UX design, including user research and design thinking.

  • Textbook: "Don't Make Me Think" by Steve Krug
  • YouTube: Intro to UX Design by UX Mastery

2. User Research

Learn how to conduct user research, including interviews, surveys, and usability testing.

  • Textbook: "User Research: A Practical Guide to Designing Better Products and Services" by Stephanie Marsh
  • YouTube: User Research Tutorial by NNGroup

3. Wireframing and Prototyping

Learn how to create wireframes and prototypes to visualize and test your designs.

  • Textbook: "Wireframing and Prototyping for UX Design" by Tony Fernandes
  • YouTube: Wireframing Tutorial by CareerFoundry

4. Interaction Design

Understand the principles of interaction design and how to create intuitive user interfaces.

  • Textbook: "Designing Interactions" by Bill Moggridge
  • YouTube: Interaction Design Tutorial by Jesse Showalter

5. Visual Design

Learn the basics of visual design, including color theory, typography, and layout.

  • Textbook: "Designing the Visual Interface: Communication Oriented Techniques" by Kevin Mullet and Darrell Sano
  • YouTube: Visual Design Basics by AJ&Smart

6. UX Design Tools

Get proficient in using popular UX design tools like Sketch, Figma, and Adobe XD.

  • Textbook: "UX Design Tools: Foundations & Practices" by Krispian Emert
  • YouTube: Figma Tutorial by DesignCourse
  • YouTube: Adobe XD Tutorial by DesignCourse

7. Usability Testing

Learn how to conduct usability testing to gather feedback and improve your designs.

  • Textbook: "Handbook of Usability Testing" by Jeffrey Rubin and Dana Chisnell
  • YouTube: Usability Testing Tutorial by UX Mastery

8. Accessibility

Understand the importance of accessibility in UX design and how to make your designs accessible to all users.

  • Textbook: "Accessibility Handbook" by Katie Cunningham
  • YouTube: Accessibility in UX Design by Google Chrome Developers
