CRM Technical Developer Roadmap

Your guide to becoming a successful CRM technical developer

1. Fundamentals of Programming

Start by learning the fundamental concepts of programming including variables, loops, and conditionals.

  • Textbook: "Learn to Program" by Chris Pine
  • YouTube: Programming Basics by freeCodeCamp

2. Data Structures and Algorithms

Learn about different data structures (arrays, linked lists, trees, etc.) and algorithms to manipulate them.

  • Textbook: "Introduction to Algorithms" by Thomas H. Cormen
  • YouTube: Data Structures and Algorithms Course by mycodeschool

3. CRM Fundamentals

Understand the basics of Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and its importance in business.

  • Textbook: "CRM at the Speed of Light" by Paul Greenberg
  • YouTube: Introduction to CRM by SuperOffice

4. CRM Platforms

Get familiar with popular CRM platforms like Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and HubSpot.

  • Textbook: "Learning Salesforce Visual Workflow" by Rakesh Gupta
  • YouTube: Salesforce for Beginners by Salesforce
  • Textbook: "Mastering Microsoft Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement" by Deepesh Somani
  • YouTube: Microsoft Dynamics 365 Tutorial by Dynamics 365 Academy

5. CRM Customization and Configuration

Learn how to customize and configure CRM platforms to meet business needs.

  • Textbook: "Microsoft Dynamics CRM Customization Essentials" by Nicolae Tarla
  • YouTube: CRM Customization and Configuration by Dynamics 365 Academy

6. CRM Development

Dive into developing custom solutions and integrations for CRM platforms.

  • Textbook: "Advanced Apex Programming in Salesforce" by Dan Appleman
  • YouTube: CRM Development by Salesforce Developers

7. Integration and API Development

Learn how to integrate CRM platforms with other systems and develop APIs.

  • Textbook: "Salesforce Lightning Platform Enterprise Architecture" by Andrew Fawcett
  • YouTube: RESTful API Tutorial by Traversy Media

8. Testing and Deployment

Understand best practices for testing CRM solutions and deploying them to production.

  • Textbook: "Testing and Test-Driven Development in Salesforce" by Alex Sutherland
  • YouTube: Salesforce Deployment Best Practices by Salesforce Developers
